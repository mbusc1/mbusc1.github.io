Why Hire Matt?

I like to get straight to the point: why should you hire me? What set me apart from other potential candidates? This how I would sum it up:

Extensive coding knowledge

UC Irvine's Informatics program, my work and personal tinkering have taught me a lot of different coding languages, I have used all of these languages at some point to varying degrees, and I'm always learning:

Python, Node.js, Angular, Ionic, Drupal, Wordpress, Unity/C#, Stripe, HTML5, CSS, Linux terminal and SSH, Apache, Android Studio, Lua, Arduino Microcontroller, Mysql, Java, JSP, C, C++, Curl

Adaptability

I have a lot of passion for games and programming, so I'm always willing to learn and test new frameworks, dig through the documentation, and get right into work.

User Focus

As part of my work as a web developer, and as part of my studies in Human Computer Interaction, I have a strong focus on creating sustainable and usable software. I also feel very confident in my ability to perform QA to ensure this.

Appreciation for art and design

While they aren't my best skillset, good design and aesthetic I find essential to making a polished, professional product. When I code, I perform QA and work with design and project management teams to create high quality and usable content.

Team Player

I love to socialize, chat with people, and work together on projects. Don't believe me? Ask me for an in person or phone interview!

Passionate

Programming and Gaming are what I truly love to work on and create, and I will always give 100% to what I am passionate about.
